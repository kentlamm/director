I'm Kent, and I've been creating film & video content since age 11.

While a craetive writing undergrad at University of Southern California, I directed my first feature length film, Bad is Bad. Taking advantage of local resources in my hometown of Richmond, VA, I stretched the $6,000 budget to create a profitable, award-winning film with over 3 million views online. It was one of the first features to be shot entirely on DSLR, and distributed for free on YouTube and Vimeo.

I love working, so I've stayed busy since: -Another micro-budget feature in college
-Won a national video contest for HotSchedules, and was hired to make several more videos for their affiliate companies
-Created 8 short films in 8 months for my Youtube channel, Standard Story Company
-Directed & edited the first season of Jeff's Place, a webseries which won Best Comedic Actor (Jeff Galfer) and Best Editor (myself) at the NY TV Festival
-And much more

As an editor, I've done work for brands such as Netflix, Air Jordan, Woman's Day, Peter Nappi Shoes, 5Why, etc. I love finding new techniques and styles in post to make videos that stand out and grab viewers.

I live in Los Angeles, where I also enjoy film photography, guitar, chess, coffee, and dogs.
