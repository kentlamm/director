hello



I'm Kent, and I've been creating film & video content since age 11.

While studying creative writing at the University of Southern California, I directed my first feature length film, Bad is Bad. Taking advantage of local resources in my hometown of Richmond, VA, I stretched the $6,000 budget to create a profitable, award-winning film with over 3 million views online. It was one of the first features to be shot entirely on DSLR, and distributed for free on YouTube and Vimeo.

In 2013, I won a national video contest for HotSchedules, and was hired to make several more comedic videos for their other brands.

In 2015, I wanted to test my limits for churning out stories, and created 8 short films in 8 months for my Youtube channel, Standard Story Company, while working 40hrs/week as a waiter.

In 2016, I directed and edited the first season of the webseries Jeff's Place, which won Best Comedic Actor (Jeff Galfer) and Best Editor (myself) at the NY TV Festival.

As an editor, I've done work for brands such as Netflix, Air Jordan, Woman's Day, Peter Nappi Shoes, 5Why, etc. I love finding new techniques and styles in post that make videos that stand out and engage viewers.

I live in Los Angeles, where I also enjoy film photography, guitar, chess, coffee, and dogs.
