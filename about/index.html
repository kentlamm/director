My name is Kent Lamm and I'm an award-winning LA-based freelance director and editor for film and video.

While a creative writing undergrad at University of Southern California, I directed my first feature length film, Bad is Bad. Taking advantage of local resources in my hometown of Richmond, I stretched the $6,000 budget to create a profitable, award-winning film with over 3 million views online. It was one of the first features to be shot entirely on DSLR, and distributed for free on YouTube and Vimeo.

As an editor, I've done work for brands such as Netflix, Air Jordan, Woman's Day, and more. I work fast and smart, leaving more time for the creative discoveries that elevate a video into a one-of-a-kind experience.
